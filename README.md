# The Path of Embers

A Dark Souls-inspired breathing and meditation application with progression mechanics, covenant systems, and community features.

### Core Breathing Techniques
- **Twilit Respite** (4-7-8 Breathing) - For stress and anxiety reduction
- **Iron Flesh Technique** (Box Breathing) - For focus and discipline
- **The Dragon's Roar** (Wim Hof Inspired) - For energy and vitality
- **Estus Breath** (Resonance Breathing) - For healing and restoration

### Progression System
- **Bonfire Levels**: Your dedication is visualized through evolving ember states
  - Smoldering (Days 1-3)
  - Steady Flame (Days 4-7)
  - Roaring (Days 8-14)
  - First Flame (Days 15+)
- **Streak Tracking**: Build daily streaks with a grace period system
- **Grace Period**: Miss a day? You have 2 days to restore your flame

### Covenant System
Choose your path and receive themed UI and preferred breathing techniques:
- **Covenant of Respite** (The Way of Blue) - Stress & Anxiety Reduction
- **Covenant of Focus** (The Blades of the Darkmoon) - Discipline & Productivity
- **Covenant of Vigor** (The Sunlight Warriors) - Energy & Physical Wellness

### Journaling Integration
- **Pre-Ritual Prompts**: "What burdens do you bring to the flame?"
- **Post-Ritual Reflection**: "What clarity have you found?"
- All entries are saved locally for your personal grimoire

### The Chronicler's Map
A beautiful constellation visualization of your 90-day journey, showing:
- Daily session completion as glowing bonfires
- Connected paths showing your consistency
- Detailed statistics and recent session history

### Echoes of Travelers
An asynchronous community feature inspired by Dark Souls messages:
- Leave supportive messages for other travelers
- Receive random encouraging echoes from the community
- Pre-set message templates maintain positivity
